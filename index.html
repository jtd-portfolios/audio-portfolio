01 — About

A great ear, a steady hand on the fader.

I'm an audio editor and producer who cares about how things sound — and why. My work spans post-production cleanup, multitrack mixing, sound design and original composition, with a focus on delivering clear, professional, broadcast-ready audio.

I work fluently across both destructive/waveform editors and full DAWs, and I'm comfortable with the whole chain: recording, EQ, noise reduction, spectral repair, mixing and mastering. I pay close attention to acoustics, signal flow and the small artifacts most listeners never notice — but always feel.

I'm currently looking for work where a sharp ear and meticulous editing make a real difference — including evaluating, annotating and refining audio quality at a high standard.
